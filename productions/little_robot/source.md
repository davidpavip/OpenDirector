# Creative Brief

A lonely ten-year-old boy discovers an abandoned robot in the mountains.

The robot has forgotten its original purpose. The boy repairs it, and together
they begin walking toward a mysterious glowing village in the valley.

The story should feel hopeful, emotional, and adventurous, with a quiet sense
of uncertainty.

# Visual Direction

- Cinematic 3D animation
- Golden sunset gradually changing into blue twilight
- Lush mountain valley
- Warm village lights
- Gentle, stable camera movement

# Character Requirements

- The boy is brave, curious, and kind.
- The robot is loyal, curious, and slightly clumsy.
- Their relationship is the emotional center of the story.

# Constraints

- Family-friendly
- Violence must not be glorified
- Preserve character identity throughout the production
- Prefer a small, effective story-role list
