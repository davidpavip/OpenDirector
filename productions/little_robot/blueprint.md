# Production Blueprint

**Version:** 1
**Status:** approved

## Intent

Create an intimate animated short centered on loneliness, trust, and companionship.

## Story

A lonely boy repairs an abandoned robot. As they cross the mountain valley, each protects the other in small, gentle ways. Their growing friendship gives both of them the courage to enter the village.

## Story Roles

### Boy

- **Importance:** primary
- **Purpose:** Human center of the emotional story.

### Robot

- **Importance:** primary
- **Purpose:** Companion reflecting the boy's isolation.

## Professions

### Director

Responsibilities:
- Protect the creative intent
- Approve major production decisions

### Screenwriter

Responsibilities:
- Develop story structure
- Write scene action and dialogue

### Cinematographer

Responsibilities:
- Design camera language
- Define lighting and composition

### Editor

Responsibilities:
- Shape pacing
- Protect continuity and emotional rhythm

### Animator

Responsibilities:
- Create character and environmental motion

### Composer

Responsibilities:
- Develop the musical language

## Style

- **Visual Style:** cinematic 3D animation
- **Lighting:** soft blue-gold twilight
- **Camera:** slow observational movement
- **Tone:** warm, emotional, and quietly adventurous

## Constraints

- Family-friendly
- Do not glorify violence
- Preserve character identity
- Use the smallest effective cast
- Use minimal dialogue.
- The relationship must drive every major scene.
