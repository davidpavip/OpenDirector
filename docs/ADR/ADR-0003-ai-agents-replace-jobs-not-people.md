# ADR-0003: AI agents replace jobs, not people

## Status
Accepted

## Context
OpenDirector uses AI agents to perform specialized creative roles. The risk is that automation could remove human authorship.

## Decision
AI agents replace jobs, not people.

The filmmaker remains the creative authority. AI agents automate repetitive work, explain options, and preserve creative freedom.

## Consequences
- The human creator is always the final authority.
- AI recommendations must be editable.
- AI reviews should explain reasoning.
- Features that remove creative control should be redesigned.
